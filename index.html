About Sachin Kumar

Hailing from the historic town of Doghat in the Baghpat district of Uttar Pradesh, Sachin Kumar (Pawar) is a testament to what dedication, intellect, and a kind heart can achieve. Known among his peers and community as an incredibly compassionate and grounded individual, Sachin has never settled for the ordinary.

He is a man of remarkable versatility. Throughout his life, he has turned down numerous comfortable positions in pursuit of greater challenges and wider horizons. From the corporate sector to the frontlines of law enforcement, and now in central revenue, his life is a journey of continuous evolution and unwavering commitment to serving the nation.

Academic Brilliance

Sachin’s intellectual capabilities were evident early on. He brought immense pride to his hometown by securing the position of District Topper in his Class 10th board examinations.

Building on this strong foundation, he pursued higher education in the technical field, earning a highly respected B.Tech degree from the Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET). This unique blend of technical acumen and administrative capability defines his approach to modern governance and law enforcement.

A Dynamic Career Path

Never afraid to chase a new opportunity to serve.

2011

Corporate Sector Professional

Sachin began his professional journey in the corporate world, gaining valuable organizational and management skills. However, his drive for public service eventually led him to explore government opportunities.

2018

Sub-Inspector, Uttar Pradesh Police

Dedicated to maintaining law and order, he joined the UP Police as a Sub-Inspector. He served his state with integrity and bravery, showcasing his strong leadership capabilities on the ground.

2020 – Present

Inspector, GST Department

Always seeking personal and professional growth, Sachin made the bold decision to resign from the police force to prepare for central examinations. His hard work paid off when he became an Inspector in the GST Department. After serving diligently in Panipat, Haryana, he is currently stationed in Delhi, contributing to the nation's economic framework.
